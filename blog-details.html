Select your mercenary and fight until you are the last one standing

  • byAdmin
  • 11/14/2022

Play, enjoy and prove your experience as a mercenary who deserves to be the last one standing, do not be afraid of the safe zone, be careful with what is inside that dense cloud, if you hide you will draw attention and you will be hunted, without fear, breathe and shoot straight to the skull.

Weapons, Mechanics and Gameplay

You'll know a little bit about what we'll offer in Battle Royale mode

  • Weapons: FN Scar, M4A1, M16, AWM, Grenade Launcher, BHPB 9mm and among many more.
  • You can communicate in a more interactive way with your team using microphone, directions, transfer of control, distribution of loot, if you need something you will have it, change of loot without having to be near your team.
  • Parkour, frenzy, executions, accessories, a safe area different from the rest, beware of the dense fog that there are zombies, tentacles and can drag you to hell, you want to revive you will not be able but if you will have a matchmaking a little less aggressive.

I know they are the best mercenaries, that's why we've hired them. HAYCLAVAYA is a mercenary shooter, a free-to-play massively multiplayer in which you will compete in the village of Waspam. And you will stand out fighting, you will gain experience, recognition and in the process you will get excited to lose track of time.

What will you do in zombie mode?

Zombie mode consists of 3 battle modes, which are important for you to collect money and upgrade your equipment, the key is not to die in the time we agreed between you and me. Be careful with the hordes of zombies, manage your weapons and ammunition well, be smart, try not to make mistakes, do not let fear consume you, show what you are made of in the arena, complete the mission and return home.

The story mode will take place on the Waspam map, a place consumed by hordes of zombies. you will have eight mercenaries with whom you will carry out your mission, you will have two stories in which you will decide how all this will end. You will witness the beginning and the end of the chaos and suffering in the Miskito village consumed by the Bribrit virus.

What will you do in survival mode?

Forests, trees, vegetation, animals, beasts, immersion as you will explore the forests of Central America, enjoy and explore during the day but at night it will be dangerous because you will have to survive against ghosts, witches, goblins, mermaids, demons so when it is night seek your shelter and rest.

How much will it cost?

-Free Battle Royale mode (Battle Pass 9 dollars). -Zombie Mode (60 dollars). -Survival mode (20 dollars).
